About:

All artists work in a dialog with others. The Exchange Archive supports
artistic dialog by showing the inspirations that flow between projects.

The Exchange Archive is a platform for showcasing art online,
especially works about exchange or barter.

We highlight the connections between works, to foster the art community.
To join us, add a project or just explore!
